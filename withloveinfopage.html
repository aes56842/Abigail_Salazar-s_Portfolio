With Love Market & Cafe

Role

Designer

Year

2026

Tools

Download Slides

With Love Market & Cafe is a community-led and owned initiative founded in 2014 and operating its market and cafe since 2016. With a mission to bring healthy food access to our neighborhood in South Los Angeles. While their mission is big, we found that its presence in the digital space was a but outdated. Trying to convey the cafe's beautiful atmosphere with it's heartwarming mission, we created a modern, vibrant, welcoming and simple web page for all ages to understand, freely explore and order.

In order to make this happen, I interviewed people in the surrounding South LA area and asked them about their day to day lives, their struggles and support systems to understand just how much of an impact this cafe had in it's community. Additionally these interviews served as qualitive data to understand what a user might need, what their priorities were and how we can best design around the target audience. With this in mind, I identified a general UI/UX and onboarding experience was necessary as well as a simple process to achieve their desired goal seamlessly.
